How it all started

Welcome to the River C's poker page! We are a group of friends that really like to take each others hard earned money. Winning money is fun and all but really we just needed an excuse to get away from reality and spend some time together. Our group started playing together in 2018, and half way through 2020 we decided to create a league. The league was created to try to keep consistency within the group, and to play for a bigger pot at the end of the year. At the end of 2020 our total pot was $360. Visit the players-page to see the 2020 champion and the rest of the league!

How the league works

Everyone is welcome to join in on the games that we play. However, to be apart of the league requires a little bit more commitment. The league gets together once a month and in order to compete at the end of the year players can't miss more than one game throughout the year, and each player will also need to host a game within the year. The league fee is $5 every game, or $60 up front or at the end of the year. The final league pot will vary on the number of players in the league that year, but will usually sit around $400 - $500.

Where we are from

Our whole group lives in the Ogden, Utah valley. Some of us have known eachother since highschool and some work with one another. Either way its a small city that brought us all together. Depending on the night, that can be a good thing or bad thing. Regardless of where you are from, if you are interested in playing some friendly poker consistently join our poker group on facebook!
